# Business Problem & Solution Overview


## Business Context

The organization operates within the wholesale import and distribution industry, managing large transactional sales volumes across multiple products, customers, salespeople, and geographic regions.

The business required a centralized analytics solution capable of improving visibility into sales performance, profitability, operational efficiency, and strategic product analysis.


## Business Challenges

Leadership teams faced several analytical limitations:
- Limited visibility into product profitability
- Difficulty identifying top-performing products
- Lack of centralized executive reporting
- Poor understanding of revenue concentration risk
- Limited operational performance monitoring
- Inefficient investigation workflows for detailed product analysis


## Project Objectives

The primary objectives of the solution were:
- Build an enterprise-style Power BI reporting solution
- Improve executive visibility into business performance
- Analyze product profitability trends
- Monitor operational KPIs
- Support geographic sales analysis
- Enable interactive business investigation workflows
- Implement scalable and optimized BI architecture


## Solution Approach

The solution was developed using:
- Power BI Desktop
- Power Query
- DAX
- Star schema data modeling
- Interactive dashboard design
- Drillthrough workflows
- Mobile layout optimization
- Row-Level Security (RLS)

The architecture focused on scalability, performance optimization, and executive-level usability.


## Target Business Users

The solution was designed for:
- Executive leadership teams
- Product managers
- Sales managers
- Operations analysts
- Business analysts
- Regional performance stakeholders


## Expected Business Value

The solution aimed to deliver:
- Faster executive decision-making
- Improved profitability visibility
- Enhanced product portfolio analysis
- Better operational KPI monitoring
- Stronger self-service analytics capability
- Improved business reporting efficiency

## Final Outcome

The completed solution delivered an enterprise-style analytics platform capable of supporting interactive reporting, profitability analysis, operational monitoring, and advanced business investigation workflows using Power BI.
