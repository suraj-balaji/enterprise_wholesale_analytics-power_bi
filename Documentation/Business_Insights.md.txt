# Business Insights & Recommendations

## Overview

The Power BI solution was developed to identify revenue trends, product profitability patterns, operational performance, and sales concentration risks across the wholesale distribution business.


## Revenue Concentration Analysis

Pareto analysis revealed that a relatively small number of products generated a disproportionately large share of total revenue.

Business Impact:
- High dependency on limited product categories
- Increased concentration risk
- Revenue vulnerability if key products underperform

Recommendation:
- Diversify revenue streams
- Expand promotion of mid-performing products
- Reduce dependency on top revenue-driving products


## Product Profitability Analysis

Scatter plot analysis showed that several high-revenue products generated lower profit margins compared to other products.

Business Impact:
- Revenue growth does not always translate into profitability growth
- Margin erosion risk on high-volume products

Recommendation:
- Review pricing strategies
- Negotiate supplier costs
- Improve margin monitoring for high-volume products

## Geographic Sales Performance

Sales distribution varied significantly across states and regional markets.

Business Impact:
- Certain regions contributed heavily to overall sales performance
- Geographic concentration may create regional business risk

Recommendation:
- Identify expansion opportunities in underperforming regions
- Allocate sales resources strategically
- Monitor regional growth trends continuously


## Product Segmentation Insights

Scatter plot quadrant analysis enabled segmentation of products into:
- Strategic products
- Growth opportunities
- Low-performing products
- High-risk low-margin products

Business Impact:
- Supports data-driven product portfolio decisions
- Improves prioritization of sales and marketing efforts

Recommendation:
- Focus investment on high-margin strategic products
- Reassess low-performing product lines
- Develop targeted growth strategies


## Operational Performance Insights

Delivery-related KPIs indicated relatively stable operational performance across sales periods.

Business Impact:
- Consistent delivery operations support customer satisfaction
- Operational stability improves order fulfillment reliability

Recommendation:
- Continue monitoring delivery KPIs
- Implement early warning thresholds for operational delays
